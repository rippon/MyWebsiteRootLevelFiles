Robot Arms

click here for Robot Arms demo

This app models the behaviour of two 'robot arms' that start, one folded back on the other, on the x-axis.

The user chooses and clicks a target point within the circle, and then the robot goes to that point.

Before clicking 'Go', the user has the option of clicking 'Optimise'. This will optimise the solution, i.e. the robot will proceed to the target point in the quickest, most efficient way. To do this, it first works out whether each arm should rotate clockwise or anti-clockwise, and proceeds accordingly.
